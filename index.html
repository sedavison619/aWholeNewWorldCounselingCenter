Here at a Whole New World Counseling Center you will find a patient, knowledgeable, compassionate guide
to help you find a new way forward

What can I expect
when I arrive?

You will walk into our cozy waiting room and check in with our front desk representative. If this is your very first visit you may be given some documents to fill out.

What should I
bring with me?

For the first session we ask that you bring a form of identification and your insurance card. Any other items or support persons are up to you!

Is there anything
I'm not allowed to bring?

We do not allow weapons of any kind, drugs, alcohol, animals other than service animals, or anything that would disrupt the peaceful environment of our center.

Our Services

Our counselors offer a range of services in various styles to fit the needs of our clients.

  • Individual Counseling

    Individuals seek out our services to help them grow and leave being old patterns of behavior and thinking that are no longer helpful.

  • Telemental Health

    We are able to provide counseling over virtual platorms to meet with clients where they are. --Clients are required to be located in the state of Alabama due to licenseing restrictions--

  • Children, Teens,

    We work with children as young as 8 years old.

  • Group Services

    We offer a variety of groups for both support and skill building.

  • Couples Counseling

    Our Gottman Method trained therapists specialize in marital and romantic relationships.

  • Confidentiality

    All communication with your therapist is protected and private information that cannot be shared with anyone without your written consent.
